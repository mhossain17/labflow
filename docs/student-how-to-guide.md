# LabFlow Student How-To Guide

This guide walks students through using LabFlow from account setup to final lab submission.

## Quick Start Checklist

- Get your school code from your teacher.
- Create your account (or sign in if you already have one).
- Open **My Labs** and select your assigned lab.
- Complete the **Pre-Lab** questions.
- Work through each step, enter data, and mark steps complete.
- Submit the lab on the completion page.

## 1. Create Your Account (First Time Only)

1. Go to the sign-up page.
2. Enter your first name, last name, school email, password, and school code.
3. Submit the form.
4. Check your email and confirm your account.
5. Return to LabFlow and sign in.

## 2. Sign In and Open Your Labs

1. Sign in with your email and password.
2. You will land on **My Labs**.
3. Each lab card shows the lab title, current status, due date (if added), and progress.
4. Click **Continue** (or **View** for completed labs).

## 3. Review the Lab Overview

On the overview page, read:

- Overview and learning objectives
- Materials list
- Safety notes
- Estimated time

Then click **Start Pre-Lab** or **Continue**.

## 4. Complete Pre-Lab Questions

1. Answer each question before starting the lab procedure.
2. Questions marked with `*` are required.
3. Click **Submit Pre-Lab**.
4. You will be taken to Step 1.

## 5. Complete Lab Steps

For each step:

1. Read the step instructions.
2. Enter required data values.
3. Add reflection text if prompted.
4. Watch save feedback under Data Entry: `Saving...`, `Saved`, or `Failed to save. Please try again.`
5. Click **Mark Step Complete**.
6. Continue to the next step.

Notes:

- Required fields must be completed before a step can be marked complete.
- If a value is outside expected range, you may see a warning message. Review and correct if needed.
- On the final step, use **Mark Complete & Finish**.

## 6. Use the Right Sidebar While Working

The sidebar helps you stay organized during a lab run:

- **My Status**: set your current status.
- **Progress**: shows pre-lab/step progress.
- **Quick Note**: add observations or questions (auto-saves).

Status options:

- On Track
- Need Help
- Stuck
- Waiting for Teacher
- Finished This Step

## 7. Ask for Help

On step pages, you may see **Get Help on This Step**.

1. Open help.
2. If troubleshooting steps are provided, try those first.
3. Continue to AI chat for guided help.
4. If still blocked, request teacher help when prompted.

Important:

- The AI assistant is designed to guide you with questions, not give direct answers.

## 8. Submit Your Lab

After all steps are complete:

1. Open the completion page.
2. Review your step summary.
3. Click **Submit Lab**.

After submission, you will see:

- **Lab Complete!**
- Completion date/time
- A summary of completed steps

## 9. Update Your Profile and Appearance

From your avatar menu (top right), you can open:

- **Account Settings**: update first and last name.
- **Appearance**: choose Light, Dark, or System theme.
- **Sign Out**: safely log out.

## 10. Common Issues and Fixes

**I don’t see any labs**

- Your teacher may not have assigned one yet. Check back or ask your teacher.

**I can’t start a step**

- Complete all required pre-lab questions first.

**My step won’t mark complete**

- Check for missing required data fields.

**The help button is missing**

- Help appears on step pages when help chat is enabled for your class.

**I forgot my password**

- Use **Forgot password?** on the sign-in page to request a reset email.
