## Timestamped Script

### 00:00-00:18 | Downtime Disrupts Momentum
In many classrooms, valuable minutes are lost while students wait for direction, repeat questions, or pause between lab steps.

### 00:18-00:38 | Limited Step-Level Visibility
Teachers often cannot see, in real time, who is progressing, who is stuck, and which step is creating friction. That visibility gap drives downtime and uneven support.

### 00:38-00:50 | What Was Built
LabFlow was built to close that gap with one connected workflow for planning, student work, and live instructional awareness.

### 00:50-01:02 | Value Proposition
It gives teachers and administrators faster access to student steps, data, and trends, so class time is used for coaching and learning instead of manual tracking.

### 01:02-01:22 | Teacher Lab Builder
Teachers can generate a full lab draft, refine instructions, and publish with clear objectives, materials, and grading criteria in one flow.

### 01:22-01:42 | Student Pre-Lab Workflow
Students open assigned labs, complete pre-lab thinking, and move through structured steps that build reasoning through guided data collection.

### 01:42-02:02 | Data Entry and Reflection
At each step, students record observations and reflect on evidence. Work is continuously captured, making progress visible without stopping instruction.

### 02:02-02:18 | When a Student Gets Stuck
When a student gets stuck, guided help appears in context, and the teacher can still stay in control of instructional decisions.

### 02:18-02:28 | Live Monitor and Grouping
From the live monitor, teachers can see status by student and by step, then quickly group learners for targeted support.

### 02:28-02:38 | Analytics for Leaders
Administrators and instructional teams can review classroom analytics to spot patterns and support coaching conversations.

### 02:38-02:52 | 30-Day Pilot Plan
A practical adoption plan starts with a 30-day pilot in two to three high school or higher classes that rely on step-based, data-rich work. Track completion rate, help-response time, and grading time saved.
